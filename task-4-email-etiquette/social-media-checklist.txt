Social Media DO’s and DON’Ts for College Students

DO’s:
•	Keep Privacy Settings Updated: Make sure you have updated your social media settings so you know who can see your information.
•	Think Before You Post: Make sure you are projecting a professional image.
•	Verify Information: Make sure you are not spreading false information.
•	Network Professionally: Use LinkedIn to connect with industry leaders and academic groups related to AI/ML.
•	Use Strong Passwords: Make sure you have Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) enabled on all your social media accounts.

DON’Ts:
•	Don’t Share Sensitive Info: Make sure you never post your registration number, home address, or OTP on social media.
•	Don’t Engage in Cyberbullying: Make sure you avoid posting rumors, hate speech, or inflammatory comments on social media.
•	Don’t Plagiarize Content: Make sure you avoid posting other people’s creative works without proper citations.
•	Don’t Over-Post During Class: Make sure you avoid getting distracted by social media.
•	Don’t Use Offensive Language: Make sure you avoid hate speech or profanity, as it could get you into trouble with your college administration.
